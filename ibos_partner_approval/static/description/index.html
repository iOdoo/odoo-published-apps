ODOO Partner Approval

Partner Approval module is made to allow you to approve or reject contacts only by the contact manager. It also triggers or enables easy search or filter contact by state including Draft, Under Approval, Approved or Not Approved. It’s totally up to the managers whether they’re going to give approval, leave it under process, keep it under hold, or reject it.
